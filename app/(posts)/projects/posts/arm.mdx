### Brand Design

A new visual identity designed to unify Arm's diverse product lines and strengthen its market position. This transformation aimed to create a more cohesive and recognizable identity across all platforms and products.

<ImageGroup>
  <Image src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmcisted7/Nova/main/Screenshot%202025-06-16%20at%2008.28.50.png" alt="Arm branding elements" fill />
  <Image src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmcisted7/Nova/main/Screenshot%202025-06-16%20at%2008.28.50.png" alt="Arm applications and ecosystem" fill />
</ImageGroup>

The redesign focused on creating a flexible visual system that could adapt to various applications, from consumer-facing devices to enterprise solutions. The updated visual language emphasizes clarity, consistency, and a forward-thinking spirit, embodying the core values of the Arm ecosystem.

<Image src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmcisted7/Nova/main/Screenshot%202025-06-16%20at%2008.28.50.png" alt="Arm brand imagery" variant="full" />

Central to the new brand identity is the evolution of its core visual assets. The refreshed design gives more personality and versatility, allowing it to be expressed in various forms and contexts while remaining instantly recognizable.

  <Image src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmcisted7/Nova/main/Screenshot%202025-06-16%20at%2008.28.50.png" alt="Arm visual system creation" fill />

The comprehensive brand rollout included guidelines for typography, color palettes, and animation principles, ensuring that the Arm brand is consistently represented across all touchpoints. This meticulous attention to detail has resulted in a brand that is both modern and timeless.
